The next steps of the job system

Not a lot was done over the day, but these are some of the changes I did.
The chair manafucturing process started. At first the chairs were made of a single rock, a dwarf would take one rock from the quarry and place it into the chair factory. Then I made it so that chairs would be made from 4 rocks (2 was a step to there) and the dwarfs would need to take 4 rocks from the quarry to make a chair.
Because there was only 3 chairs wanting to be made, in the system where only 1 dwarf would do a job node each time, it was taking some times for the dwarfs to make a chair. I decided to start working on that communication system to make it faster.
I made a storage where the dwarfs would bring all of their goods to there after collecting them. then, when a dwarf looks for a job node, if the storage has the needed materials, they can walk there and get the materials needed for the job.
It was good but it still meant that several dwarfs were waiting near the storage area for their turn to collect the rocks. I changed the system to have several dwarfs taking rocks at the same time, and the dwarfs quickly settled into what appears to be a good configuration that can be seen here.
